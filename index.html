Introduction

A long time ago ...

Mankind sent a space ship to a galaxy far far away to a planet called Utopia named after a village on planet earth.

Decades later people have colonised across this planet and find different areas to cultivate and live in. These slides provide statistical information about how the population has developed in key areas between January 2015 and June 2016 and what the current trends are.

The Shiny application allows you to explore all available regions on planet Utopia individually or compare with other regions not presented here.
